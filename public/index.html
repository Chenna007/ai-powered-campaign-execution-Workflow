⚙ How It Works

What Runs In The Background

A practical system map showing how each stage drives better campaign output quality.

1. Input To Strategy

The engine validates business context first, then creates a strategy with channels, tactics, KPIs, and budget intent alignment.

  • Business objective parsing
  • Audience-to-channel mapping
  • KPI framework generation

2. Strategy To Execution Assets

Once strategy is approved, the platform compiles creator shortlist, outreach drafts, and timeline blocks for launch readiness.

  • Creator ranking and fit score
  • Personalized DM + email drafts
  • Operational timeline construction

3. Execution To Reporting

During and after execution, the workflow converts campaign activity into projected performance and ROI-ready reporting for leadership.

  • Performance signal aggregation
  • Conversion and cost projection
  • CEO-ready report formatting

Best Output Checklist

To get the strongest campaign output, follow this sequence every time:

  1. Define one primary campaign goal
  2. Specify audience clearly with location and age
  3. Set realistic budget and timeline constraints
  4. Review AI strategy before outreach starts
  5. Track KPI deltas daily and iterate quickly
